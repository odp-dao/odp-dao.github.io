Contribute to 'Open Delivery Platform'

The 'Open Delivery Platform' project is under constant development and needs help on several fronts:

Coding

As we are an open source project, of course you can and we encourage you to contribute by submitting a pull-request in our repository. Any contribution is important to us.

Finding bugs

Help us find bugs by creating and managing stores, minting and updating NFTs, and tracking orders. Report a bug in the 'Issues' tab.

Governance

The 'Open Delivery Platform' project is a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization). Create proposals, evaluate and vote on community proposals using the voting power of your ODPT tokens. Get ODPT tokens through AIRDROP (for platform users who have one or more NFT) or by purchasing.

Donating

If you appreciate our work and/or it is useful to you, please consider donating to our DAO. The use of donated resources is done through the treasury of our governance smart contract, giving full transparency.
