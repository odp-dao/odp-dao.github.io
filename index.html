Features

How can 'Open Delivery Platform' help your business today?

Checkout Cart

Our check out cart feature is in par with any other e-commerce solution in the market .This feature helps the user to skim through the final menu.

Easy search

Our instant search feature helps the user who is looking for specific items to quickly spot it out and place orders in no time!

Track Orders

The users get status at each phase of the order, from order confirmation to delivery pickup to successful delivery. The restaurant gets a notification when an order is placed by the user.

Order Details

The restaurant owners can view the order requests, and update the order status to New orders , Confirmed orders, picked up orders, cancelled orders, denied orders etc.

Backend agnostic

Unstoppable

how it works?

Understand how we eliminate infrastructure costs and deliver true ownership

The Open Delivery platform does not own or maintain any datacenter, vps and/or servers. Instead, each merchant running the ManagerApp in their store contributes by providing a decentralized infrastructure, which includes a full IPFS node.

1. The AppData is stored in IPFS (using js-ipfs).
2. then, pinned with CrustNetwork (using W3Auth).
3. The resulting IPFS HASH is stored in blockchain (polygon) smart-contract ERC721 Standard token.
An NFT (non fungible token) that you can trade freely on any market, like Opensea for example.

NFT_ Stores Marketplace

In the marketplace you can sell your own store, or buy a ready-made store, with customers and revenue. Since every store on the ODP is an ERC721 standard NFT, your stores can be sold on any supported marketplace, such as OpenSea, for example.
